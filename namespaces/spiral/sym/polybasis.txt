SINGULAR

U = (x^(k+1) - x^(-k-1)) / (x - x^(-1))

Mapping this on to x^8+1 we get 
U-basis (x^(k+1) + x^(n-k-1)) / (x + x^(n-1))

Let n=8
First compute the inverse of (x+x^7). 1/(x+x^7) mod (x^8+1)

> reduce(1/2*(-x-x3-x5-x7)*(x+x7), std(i));
1

now we multiply by x^(k+1) + x^(8-k-1) to get all elements of the basis

> reduce(1/2*(-x-x3-x5-x7)*(x2+x6), std(i));
-x7+x
> reduce(1/2*(-x-x3-x5-x7)*(x3+x5), std(i));
-x6+x2+1
> reduce(1/2*(-x-x3-x5-x7)*(2x4), std(i));
-x7-x5+x3+x
> 

the full basis for PolyBRDFT3 via DST3 x I is given by
(U, xU)
